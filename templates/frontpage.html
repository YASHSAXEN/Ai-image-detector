How it works

Upload Image

Users upload an image in formats like JPEG or PNG

Probability Calculation

The image is resized and normalized to prepare it for analysis. The model outputs a probability score indicating the likelihood of the image being AI-generated.

Receive

The probability score and a classification label ("AI-Generated" or "Real") are shown to the user.

AI-Image Detector for your team

The AI Image Detector, created by the IIT Mandi iHub and HCI Foundation, empowers users to detect whether an image is AI-generated or real. By providing a probabilistic output, this tool aids in enhancing digital content authenticity and integrity, helping users make informed decisions in an increasingly AI-driven world.
